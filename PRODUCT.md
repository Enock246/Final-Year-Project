# Product

## Register

product

## Users
University students (specifically Aamusted students) who are anxious about finding a mandatory internship placement. They are likely stressed, overwhelmed by options, or worried about the administrative burden of securing a school. They need a guided, reassuring, and magical experience that replaces friction with delight.

## Product Purpose
To serve as an intelligent, friction-free internship finder that matches students with the right schools, generates professional application letters using AI, and tracks their application status. Success looks like students finding a placement quickly and feeling relieved and empowered by the process.

## Brand Personality
Playful, Vibrant, Reassuring. 
The aesthetic uses "Claymorphism" to provide a highly tactile, physical, and alive interface that responds to user interactions, making the digital experience feel grounded and magical.

## Anti-references
- **Dull SaaS**: No flat, gray-on-gray sterile enterprise software aesthetics.
- **Generic Data**: No endless grid of identical cards or dense, boring data tables.
- **Brutalist/Technical**: No overly complex, aggressive, or developer-centric interfaces.
- **Childish**: While playful, it must remain respectful of university students (no overly cartoonish or juvenile aesthetics).

## Design Principles
1. **Alive and Tactile**: Every element should respond to interaction. Buttons should squish, elements should track the mouse, and the interface should feel physical.
2. **Reassure through Delight**: Use motion, vibrant colors, and micro-interactions to lower student anxiety and make the process feel easy and magical.
3. **Guide, Don't Overwhelm**: Present information progressively. Avoid dumping dense data grids; instead, craft guided onboarding flows and focused single-task screens.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard WCAG AA compliance (ensure good contrast, keyboard navigability). Because the interface relies heavily on motion and 3D effects, a robust `prefers-reduced-motion` fallback is strictly required for all animations.
